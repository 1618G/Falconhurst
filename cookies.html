1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use on the Falconhurst Holiday Let website (falconhurstbay.co.uk), why we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preferences
  • Understand how visitors use our website (with your consent)
  • Improve the performance and usability of our site

We do not use cookies for advertising, remarketing, or tracking across other websites.
