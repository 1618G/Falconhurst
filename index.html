About Us

Rooms

Each of the three bedrooms have a double or king size bed. There is a large modern family bathroom, one en suite shower room and one toilet. A brand new modern kitchen has a dining area with a view. The lounge is spacious with good sea views.

Amenities

Parking is an issue in the popular village but Falconhurst has a drive that can accommodate two cars. Free Internet is through the Bay Broadband cooperative that Cliff helped to set up. Each guest will have a personal information pack full of recommendations and local knowledge.

The Village

Robin Hoods Bay

Nestled on the North Yorkshire coast, Robin Hood's Bay is one of the most picturesque fishing villages in England. With its narrow cobbled streets, traditional cottages, and stunning coastal walks, it's the perfect destination for a peaceful family getaway.

  • Historic fishing village charm
  • Beautiful coastal walks & Cleveland Way
  • Rock pools & sandy beach
  • Near Whitby & North York Moors
